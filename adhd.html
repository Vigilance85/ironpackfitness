No. The system is written for anyone whose brain works the way ADHD brains work — whether diagnosed, suspected, or simply someone who has always struggled with the consistency and initiation challenges this system addresses. Is this appropriate for teenagers with ADHD? +
Yes. The system is written for adults but the principles — anchor-based motivation, morning protocols, rescue workouts — apply directly to ADHD teenagers. Several buyers have purchased specifically for their children. The content is age-appropriate and the workouts are bodyweight-friendly, requiring no equipment.
Do I need a gym membership? +
No. The rescue workouts are fully bodyweight and home-based. The main guide includes gym programming but also outlines outdoor and minimal-equipment options. The morning anchor protocol requires nothing but the ability to walk out your front door.
Will this conflict with my ADHD medication? +
This is a fitness and nutrition guide, not medical advice. Exercise is widely supported as a complement to ADHD medication — not a replacement. The meal plan section on fasting and supplement timing should be discussed with your prescribing physician if you take stimulant medication, as timing matters. Always consult your doctor before making significant dietary or lifestyle changes.
